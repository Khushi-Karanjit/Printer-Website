SKAYK Overview


Welcome to SKAYK Collection, where innovation meets precision in the world of printing solutions. Established in 2023, we take pride in our commitment to delivering cutting-edge printers designed to meet the evolving needs of businesses and individuals alike.


Company History:

Founded in 2023 by MR.Kushal Shrestha, SKAYK Collection has grown from a vision to a thriving entity known for its dedication to excellence. Over the years, we've achieved significant milestones, thanks to the hard work and passion of our talented team. From our humble beginnings to our current standing, our journey has been marked by innovation, adaptability, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Quality and Innovation:

Our commitment to quality is evident in every product we offer. Skayk Collection is synonymous with precision, reliability, and advanced technology. We continually invest in research and development to bring forth printers that not only meet industry standards but set new benchmarks.

Future Vision:

The journey doesn't stop here. At Skayk, we are constantly looking ahead, anticipating the future needs of our customers. Our vision is to remain a leader in the printing industry, pushing boundaries and introducing innovations that redefine the printing experience. Thank you for choosing SKAYK. We look forward to being your trusted partner on your printing journey.
